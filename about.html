Our Story

A passion for authentic Italian cuisine since 2010

From Tuscany to New York

Bella Cucina began as a dream in the rolling hills of Tuscany, where our founder, Chef Marco Rossi, learned the art of Italian cooking from his grandmother. Growing up in a small village near Florence, Marco was surrounded by the aromas of fresh pasta, slow-simmered ragÃ¹, and wood-fired bread.

In 2010, Marco brought his family's recipes and culinary traditions to New York City, opening Bella Cucina in the heart of Manhattan. His vision was simple: create an authentic Italian dining experience that transports guests to the rustic trattorias of Italy.

Today, Bella Cucina stands as a testament to traditional Italian cooking, where every dish is prepared with the same care and attention that Marco learned in his grandmother's kitchen.
